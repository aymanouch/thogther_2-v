information

We are a young team of dental technicians and doctors who love their profession and are always dedicated to providing the best. We provide all kinds of patient needs from crown and bridge to full transplant.

In 2004 we opened our first center in Damascus, and after achieving great success, we started expanding to open an office for us in Austria to develop our work and expand towards the global market, then we went to the Arab and African market and opened our branch in Cairo.

Today we can work with dentists from countries around the world and thanks to the new technologies and the development of our capabilities permanently we can provide the best solutions for dentists without any problems or obstacles.

Let us guarantee comfort, for example, to the patient and the doctor alike The size of our laboratory allows us to respond quickly and flexibly to the wishes of dentists. We are ready to provide support and advice at all times and stand by you to get the best results.

Each of our technicians works from start to finish to ensure the flow of work and its quality, and this is the only and best way to achieve the best standards and high quality.
